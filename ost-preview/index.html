About The Show

A 100% focus on Human Factors — the Final Frontier of aviation safety.

On Second Thought is a monthly, live-streamed aviation safety video series. Each hour-long episode dissects the real-world human-factor behaviors behind the decisions that put pilots, maintainers, and controllers into trouble — and offers practical, research-backed ways to do better next time.

Every episode is built on peer-reviewed research from the FAA, NTSB, and international safety studies, paired with expert analysis and interactive learning that earns you free WINGS / AMT / IA credit.

Notes for John — what changed & why

  • Hero pushes Episode 5 front and center — the most time-sensitive action (watch live or set a reminder) is now the first thing a visitor sees.
  • Sticky top nav with destinations — Watch Live, Archives, Podcast, Hosts, Sponsors, and Shop. All the things currently buried inside an episode page are now one click from anywhere.
  • Archives got a dedicated section — a grid of Episodes 1–4 with proper thumbnails, Watch / Listen / Resources actions, and room to grow as the season fills in.
  • Upcoming season strip — eight future episodes as a scannable calendar strip, each with a "Remind me" tap rather than the same three buttons repeated on every card.
  • "The Hosts" is now a proper section — bigger photos, cleaner bios, and inline buttons to both hosts' other sites.
  • Dedicated JolieLucas.com feature — full-width card with the Pilot Plus One / Right Seat Ready logo, a four-card grid of her offerings (RSR!, Pilot Plus One, Speaking, One-to-One Coaching), and a clear "Visit JolieLucas.com" CTA.
  • "Coming Soon from Flying M" feature — a parallel full-width card below Jolie's, previewing Second Opinion (beta) and 103ready.com under the Flying M umbrella with a link to flyingm.aero. Same layout treatment as Jolie's feature for visual consistency.
  • Sponsors get appropriate hierarchy — Avemco as the primary sponsor feature with the 5% listener discount called out, then Lightspeed, WINGS Industry Network, and a "become a sponsor" slot below.
  • Podcast platforms are explicit — dedicated strip with Apple, Spotify, Amazon, YouTube, RSS so audio-only listeners can subscribe in one click.
  • Merch strip — small, dark, fun band linking to ostmerch.printify.me so the shop is visible without hijacking the page.
  • Single, clear newsletter signup — one prominent call-to-action at the bottom, replacing the "Sign up for Newsletter" link that repeats on every episode card today.
  • Brand preserved — steel-blue/teal primary, cream background, red ribbon accent, and the existing "Human Factors. Real Flights. Safer Skies." tagline all carry forward so it still looks like OST, just more organized.
