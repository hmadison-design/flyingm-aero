Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026  ·  Applies to: Second Opinion iOS app

Short version: Second Opinion stores all of your data on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal information or assessment results with anyone — ever.

Who We Are

Flying M (Harvey Madison, sole proprietor) is the developer of the Second Opinion iOS application. We are based in Austin, Texas, USA.

Questions about this policy may be directed to: second.opinion@flyingm.aero

What Data the App Collects

When you use Second Opinion, the following information may be stored locally on your device:

  • Pilot profile information you voluntarily enter (name/callsign, home airport, flight hours, aircraft type, tail number, flying club, certificates held)
  • Cognitive assessment results (reaction time, Stroop accuracy, working memory scores) and timestamps
  • Flight Risk Assessment Tool (FRAT) responses and scores
  • Your personal baseline preferences and app settings

All of the above is stored using your device's local storage (localStorage). None of it is transmitted off your device.

What Data We Do NOT Collect

Flying M does not collect, receive, store, or process any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your assessment results or FRAT responses
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • Usage analytics or crash telemetry (as of this version)
  • Any data through third-party analytics services (we use none)

We do not use advertising networks, marketing trackers, or any third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Location Data

The app requests access to your device's location only to display your approximate airport or city in the FRAT report header. Location data is used in-memory for this purpose only and is never stored persistently or transmitted.

You may deny location permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services. The app will continue to function normally without location access.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, license, or share your data with any third party, including advertisers, data brokers, or government agencies, except as required by applicable law.

Because your data never leaves your device, Flying M has no user data to share even if compelled to do so.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained on your device until you delete it. You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear All Data" option in the app (if available), or
  • Deleting the Second Opinion app from your device, which removes all associated local storage

Flying M does not hold copies of your data and cannot restore data deleted from your device.

Children's Privacy

Second Opinion is intended for use by certificated or student pilots and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Not a Medical Device

Second Opinion is a wellness and decision-support tool only. It is not a medical device, is not FDA-cleared or approved, and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. No medical or clinical data is processed by this app.

Security

Because your data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted, it is protected by the same security mechanisms that protect your device itself (device passcode, Face ID/Touch ID, iOS app sandboxing). We have no servers that could be breached to expose your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Material changes will also be noted in the App Store release notes. Continued use of the app after a policy update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
